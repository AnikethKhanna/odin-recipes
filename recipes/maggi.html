Spicy Maggi + Peas

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 piece Special Masala Maggi noodles + Packet
  • 1 cup green peas (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 cup water
  • Salt to taste
  • Chilli powder to taste

Steps

  1. Boil the water in a pan.
  2. Add salt and chilli powder to taste.
  3. Add the green peas to the boiling water and cook for 2-3 minutes
  4. Add the Maggi noodles and the tastemaker (spice mix) from the packet to the boiling water.
  5. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the noodles are soft and the water is mostly absorbed.
  6. Stir well to combine and ensure the noodles are evenly coated with the spices
  7. Serve hot and enjoy your spicy Maggi with peas!
